BASIC SLEEVE FOUNDATION size cm.

Note: A-B use the formula 1/2 size, not 1/2 size - 1 cm (t'as mis le - 1 entre parenthèse)

Note: A-E use the formula 1/10 height - 1 cm, not 1/10 height (t'as ajouté un - 1 cm)

The sleeve foundation is based upon the basic bodice, whose armhole circ. must be accurately taken. In order to ensure a perfect fit of the sleeve, and at the same time to ease movements, it is necessary to add 3-5 cm to the armhole circ.
